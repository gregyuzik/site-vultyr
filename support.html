FAQ

How often does Chirpless check service status?

While the app is open: every 5 minutes (configurable in Settings). The app also performs periodic background checks when conditions allow, but iOS manages the timing of these.

Why don't I get notifications when the app is closed?

Chirpless currently checks status directly from your device. Notifications work while the app is in the foreground, with periodic background checks managed by iOS. Real-time push notifications via a dedicated server are planned for a future update.

Why do some services say "Link only"?

Some services don't have a public status API. For these, Chirpless links directly to their official status page so you can check manually. Services with APIs show live status, components, and incident details automatically.

Does Chirpless collect my data?

No. All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I sync my services across devices?

Enable iCloud Sync in Settings. Your watched services, starred items, and order will sync across iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch via iCloud.

Can I request a new service?

Yes! Email greg@legendapps.dev with the service name and its status page URL.
