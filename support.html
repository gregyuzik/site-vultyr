FAQ

How often does Vultyr check service status?

On Mac: as often as every minute (configurable). On iPhone: every 5 minutes while the app is open. iOS also performs periodic background checks when conditions allow.

Can my Mac send notifications to my iPhone?

Yes! Enable "Push to iPhone" in Mac settings and import your APNS auth key. Your Mac will send push notifications directly to your iPhone when a service status changes — no external server needed.

How do I set up Mac-to-iPhone push notifications?

This is a one-time setup for developers who have an Apple Developer account:

1. Go to App Store Connect → Keys
2. Click the + button to create a new key
3. Name it anything (e.g. "vultyr push")
4. Select "Admin" access
5. Download the .p8 file
6. In vultyr on Mac, open Settings → Push to iPhone → Import Auth Key
7. Select the .p8 file you downloaded
8. Enable the toggle — done. The key is stored in your Mac's Keychain.

Why do some services say "Link only"?

Some services don't have a public status API. For these, Vultyr links directly to their official status page so you can check manually. Services with APIs show live status, components, and incident details automatically.

Does Vultyr collect my data?

No. All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I sync my services across devices?

Enable iCloud Sync in Settings. Your watched services, starred items, and order will sync across iPhone and Mac.

Can I request a new service?

Yes! Email support@vultyr.app with the service name and its status page URL.

What are the theme options?

System (default), Green Phosphor, or Amber Phosphor. Change it in Settings.
