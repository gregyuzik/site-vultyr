FAQ

How often does vultyr check service status?

On Mac: as often as every minute (configurable). On iPhone: every 5 minutes while the app is open. iOS also performs periodic background checks when conditions allow.

How do Cross-Device Alerts work?

When your Mac detects a status change, it writes a record to CloudKit (Apple's iCloud infrastructure). Your iPhone receives a push notification automatically — no keys, no tokens, no setup. Just enable "Cross-Device Alerts" in settings on either device. Keep the Mac app running for real-time monitoring.

What is AI Status Parsing?

An experimental feature that uses Apple Intelligence to parse status pages that don't have public APIs. All processing happens on-device. You can enable it for up to 5 services in Settings. Requires Apple Silicon (M1+ Mac or iPhone 15 Pro+).

Why do some services say "Link only"?

Some services don't have a public status API. For these, vultyr links directly to their official status page so you can check manually. You can optionally enable AI Status Parsing to extract status from these pages automatically.

Does vultyr collect my data?

No. All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I sync my services across devices?

Enable iCloud Sync in Settings. Your watched services, starred items, and order will sync across iPhone and Mac.

What are the theme options?

System (default), Green Phosphor, or Amber Phosphor — each with bright and dim intensity. The retro themes give the app a classic terminal aesthetic.

Can I request a new service?

Yes! Email support@vultyr.app with the service name and its status page URL.
